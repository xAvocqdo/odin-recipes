Apple Plate

A nice plate of apples that look and taste delicious, perfect with a sweet dessert like churros

Ingredients:

  • A large apple
  • Knife or 16 piece apple cutter
  • A plate

Procedure:

  1. Rinse the apple lightly
  2. Cut the apple into 16 thin pieces with either a apple cutter or a knife
  3. Check to make sure no pieces of the core or any seeds remain, if they do cut them off
  4. Arrange the apples on a plate, and enjoy
