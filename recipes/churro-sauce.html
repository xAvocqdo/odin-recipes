Churro Sauce

A nice slightly spicy sauce to go with some baked churros to enhance the taste greatly

Ingredients:

  • Some type of chocolate product
  • Some type of hot sauce or srircha
  • Microwaveable Small bowl
  • Mixing device
  • microwave

Procedure:

  1. Place the chocolate into the bowl
  2. Put the bowl into the microwave until its liquid
  3. Wait for the bowl to cool a little, then add the hot sauce or srircha to the bowl
  4. Mix gently, and you're done
